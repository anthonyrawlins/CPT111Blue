Not a local student?

RMIT considers you a local student if you are:

  • a citizen or permanent resident of Australia, or
  • a New Zealand citizen, or
  • a Temporary Protection visa (TPV) holder.

If you are unsure or hold a different visa type, please contact Info Corner for more information.

Not an international student?

RMIT considers you an international student if you are:

  • intending to study on a student visa, or
  • not a citizen or permanent resident of Australia, or
  • not a New Zealand citizen, or
  • not a Temporary Protection visa (TPV) holder.

If you are unsure or hold a different visa type, please contact Info Corner for more information.

Application to Transfer Education Provider Release Letter Form

Complete this form if you are an international student visa holder who wants to transfer from RMIT to another education provider.

Before you start

You do not need to complete this form if you have completed 6 months of your principal program of study.

Your application cannot be considered if you currently have an outstanding debt with the University.

Please allow up to 10 business days for your application to be processed. Correspondence and outcome will be provided by email.  Information collected on this form is not provided to other education providers.

Thank you.

Your submission has been received.

Please allow up to 10 business days for your application to be processed.

If your visa was issued based on a package of programs from RMIT, you may need to be released from any other programs that have not been completed.



Section 5: Supporting information

You will need to provide the following documents to support your request:
  • Valid offer from new education provider
  • Personal statement
  • Evidence to support your selection in 'Reason for transferring to another provider'
  • If you are under 18, you must also provide the documents requested in Section 2
  • If you are a sponsored student, you must also provide the documents requested in Section 3
