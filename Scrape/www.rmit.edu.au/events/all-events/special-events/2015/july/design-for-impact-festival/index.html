Not a local student?

RMIT considers you a local student if you are:

  • a citizen or permanent resident of Australia, or
  • a New Zealand citizen, or
  • a Temporary Protection visa (TPV) holder.

If you are unsure or hold a different visa type, please contact Info Corner for more information.

You’re viewing program information for international students.

Not an international student?

RMIT considers you an international student if you are:

  • intending to study on a student visa, or
  • not a citizen or permanent resident of Australia, or
  • not a New Zealand citizen, or
  • not a Temporary Protection visa (TPV) holder.

If you are unsure or hold a different visa type, please contact Info Corner for more information.

    Design for Impact Festival

    Overview

    Date: UPCOMING 23 Jul 2015 - 09 Aug 2015
    Time: UPCOMING 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM (UTC +10:00)
    Venue: EVENT CANCELLED Various locations, Melbourne
    Cost: FREE

    Design for Impact is a festival of 40 events showcasing RMIT’s design and technology research, innovation and entrepreneurship.

    The Design for Impact festival is led by the Design Research Institute (DRI), in collaboration with stakeholders across the university, and showcases RMIT’s design and technology research, innovation and entrepreneurship.

    It will engage both internally, with industry across many sectors, government and the public to help build RMIT’s collaborative opportunities for high impact research. The festival aims to be an important annual event on the RMIT industry engagement calendar.

    The festival is pleased to be included with the Open House Melbourne 2015, ShapeRMIT, Founders Day and RMIT Open Day programs with the inclusion of the international touring exhibition Smart Flexibility: Advanced Technologies and Materials by Materfad, Barcelona.
