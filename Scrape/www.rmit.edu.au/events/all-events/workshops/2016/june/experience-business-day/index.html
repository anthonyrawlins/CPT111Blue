Not a local student?

RMIT considers you a local student if you are:

  • a citizen or permanent resident of Australia, or
  • a New Zealand citizen, or
  • a Temporary Protection visa (TPV) holder.

If you are unsure or hold a different visa type, please contact Info Corner for more information.

You’re viewing program information for international students.

Not an international student?

RMIT considers you an international student if you are:

  • intending to study on a student visa, or
  • not a citizen or permanent resident of Australia, or
  • not a New Zealand citizen, or
  • not a Temporary Protection visa (TPV) holder.

If you are unsure or hold a different visa type, please contact Info Corner for more information.

Experience Business Day

Event details Event cancelled

Date: Upcoming 27 Jun 2016 - 27 Jun 2016
Time: Upcoming 9:30 AM - 3:30 PM (UTC +10:00)
Venue:

Swanston Academic Building, Building 80, Level 2, City campus
Cost: Free
More information:

Explore a day in the life of creating a business. This free workshop will take you through all the different stages from coming up with great ideas to being ready to launch.

At our Experience Business Day you'll get the opportunity to start a business and learn what is involved from an idea to the launch stage. You’ll explore these steps through interactive workshops and presentations as well as hear from our students that have gone onto create successful businesses.

Explore how major business disciplines areas such as Entrepreneurship, Management, Logistics and Supply Chain as well as Marketing and International Business all tie into the stages.
You’ll also get the chance to check out our City campus, meet other high school students and meet current RMIT Business students.

About Experience Days

RMIT University offers a number of free events designed to encourage students from years 10, 11 and 12 to engage in hands-on workshops, explore life on campus and get behind the scenes, while experiencing different aspects of discipline areas.

See a full list of available events at Experience Days.
