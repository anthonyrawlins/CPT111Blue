Not a local student?

RMIT considers you a local student if you are:

  • a citizen or permanent resident of Australia, or
  • a New Zealand citizen, or
  • a Temporary Protection visa (TPV) holder.

If you are unsure or hold a different visa type, please contact Info Corner for more information.

You’re viewing program information for international students.

Not an international student?

RMIT considers you an international student if you are:

  • intending to study on a student visa, or
  • not a citizen or permanent resident of Australia, or
  • not a New Zealand citizen, or
  • not a Temporary Protection visa (TPV) holder.

If you are unsure or hold a different visa type, please contact Info Corner for more information.

    Introducing RMIT University
    Melbourne: the world's most liveable city

    Build skills before you start a degree. The School of Global, Urban and Social Studies offers a selection of guaranteed pathways to further study.

    If you have completed one of the following programs in the RMIT School of Global, Urban and Social Studies after 2013 with a Distinction average for graded courses (GPA of 3.0) you will be guaranteed a place in one of the School’s Bachelor degrees.

    • Diploma of Languages
    • Advanced Diploma of Justice
    • Advanced Diploma of Interpreting
    • Advanced Diploma of Translating
    • Diploma of Community Services Work
    • Diploma of Community Services (Alcohol, Other Drugs & Mental Health)
    • Diploma of Community Development
    • Diploma of Youth Work
    • Advanced Diploma of Disability Work

    Your first choice Bachelor degree may not always be available. If you do not receive an offer into your first choice program, the School will ensure that you receive an offer into another School of Global, Urban and Social Studies Bachelor degree. You will be eligible for exemptions in each of the School’s Bachelor degrees.

    If you have completed the Diploma of Languages with a GPA of 3.0 you are guaranteed a place in the Bachelor of Arts (International Studies).
