Not a local student?

RMIT considers you a local student if you are:

  • a citizen or permanent resident of Australia, or
  • a New Zealand citizen, or
  • a Temporary Protection visa (TPV) holder.

If you are unsure or hold a different visa type, please contact Info Corner for more information.

You’re viewing program information for international students.

Not an international student?

RMIT considers you an international student if you are:

  • intending to study on a student visa, or
  • not a citizen or permanent resident of Australia, or
  • not a New Zealand citizen, or
  • not a Temporary Protection visa (TPV) holder.

If you are unsure or hold a different visa type, please contact Info Corner for more information.

Also in this section

    Certificates and diplomas

    Designed with industry input and directly related to specific jobs and careers. A flexible way to gain qualifications and pathways to higher level qualifications.

    Preparatory and certificates II, III and IV

    These programs will enable you to acquire operational knowledge, apply solutions to problems and analyse and evaluate information.

    Preparatory and certificates II, III and IV

    Diploma and advanced diploma

    Provide you with theoretical knowledge and/or technical and creative skills. Judgement, planning and analysis are used across a broad range of technical and/or management situations.

    Diploma and advanced diploma

    Degrees

    Professional qualifications for a higher level of work responsibility with an emphasis on theory and the development of transferable skills.

    Associate degree

    An associate degree is a two-year (full-time) university qualification that you can do after Year 12 or a certificate III or IV. You may also gain towards a related bachelor degree program.

    Associate degree

    Bachelor, double and honours degrees

    Prepare you for professional and higher management careers, promote analytical and/or technical skills and abstract thought. Double degree students can complete two awards in a reduced timeframe.

    Bachelor, double and honours degrees
